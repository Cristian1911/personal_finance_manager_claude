Sankey vs Alternatives

Sankey diagrams show money flow from sources to destinations. Popular on Reddit, but complex to build and read. Here are alternatives that convey the same insight.

Sankey Diagram

Full flow visualization: income → categories → savings

Flujo de dinero — Marzo
  • Most complete money flow visualization
  • "Wow factor" — impressive in demos
  • Shows proportional flow
  • Complex to build (no native Recharts support)
  • Hard to read on mobile
  • Confusing with many categories
  • Diminishing returns after first "aha"
